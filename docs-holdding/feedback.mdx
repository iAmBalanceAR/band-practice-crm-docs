# Feedback System

We value your input in making Band Practice CRM better. Our feedback system is designed to be straightforward and effective.

## Ways to Provide Feedback

### 1. In-App Feedback
- Use the feedback button located in the navigation menu
- Rate features and provide detailed comments
- Submit bug reports directly from relevant screens
- Suggest new features or improvements

### 2. Support Portal
- Access our dedicated support portal
- Create detailed feature requests
- Track the status of your submissions
- Engage with other users' suggestions

### 3. Community Forums
- Join discussions about features
- Share your experience
- Connect with other users
- Get tips and tricks from the community

## What Happens to Your Feedback

1. **Review**: Our team reviews all feedback within 48 hours
2. **Prioritization**: Suggestions are evaluated and prioritized
3. **Implementation**: Selected features are added to our development roadmap
4. **Updates**: You'll receive notifications about the status of your feedback

## Best Practices for Feedback

- Be specific about your suggestion or issue
- Include steps to reproduce any bugs
- Explain how the feature would benefit users
- Provide examples when possible
