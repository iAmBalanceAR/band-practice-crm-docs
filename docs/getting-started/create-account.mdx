# Create Your Account

Creating your Band Practice CRM account is quick and easy. Follow these steps to get started.

## Steps to Create Your Account

1. Visit [bandpracticecrm.com](https://bandpracticecrm.com)
2. Click the "Sign Up" button
3. Enter your email address
4. Create a secure password
5. Verify your email address

## What's Next?

After creating your account, you'll want to:
- [Set up your subscription](/getting-started/subscription-setup)
- [Complete your user profile](/getting-started/user-profile)
