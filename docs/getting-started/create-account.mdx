
# Create an Account
## Registration and Subscription
### Registration
To get started using Band Practice CRM, you need to register an account and set up your subscription..
This simple process will allow you to access all the features and benefits of the platform.
Head over to the [registration page](https://bandpracticecrm.com/auth/signup) to get started.
After filling out the form, you will receive an email with a link to verify your account.


### To Recap
1. Visit [bandpracticecrm.com](https://bandpracticecrm.com/auth/signup)
2. Fill out the short form and click the "Sign Up" button
3. Verify your email address by clicking the link in the email you received
4. Log in to your account
5. Click on billing and then Pricing
6. Click the button to subscribe, complete the payment process and you'll be redirected back to the Dashboard.
7. Now you're ready to start using Band Practice CRM! Welcome aboard!
