# Add Venue

Learn how to add new venues to your lead management system.

## Adding a New Venue

1. Navigate to Venues section
2. Click "Add New Venue"
3. Fill required information:
   - Venue name
   - Address
   - Capacity
   - Technical specs
   - Contact information

## Venue Categories

- Live Music Venues
- Bars & Clubs
- Festivals
- Private Events
- Corporate Venues
